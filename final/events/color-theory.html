Dive deep into the fascinating world of color theory with this comprehensive workshop led by renowned artists and color specialists. Learn about the psychological impact of color, harmonious color combinations, and how master painters throughout history have used color to evoke emotion and create visual impact.

This hands-on workshop includes practical exercises, color mixing demonstrations, and analysis of famous artworks. Participants will leave with a deeper understanding of how to use color effectively in their own artistic practice. All materials provided, suitable for artists of all skill levels.
